Share your WordPress posts to Microsoft Teams with a single click!

== Description ==
Teams Publisher is a WordPress plugin that allows you to easily share your blog posts to Microsoft Teams' channels.

**Features:**

* Share posts with a single click
* Choose which teams to share the post to
* Add a custom message to your shared post
* Simple and efficient:
  * Easy installation and setup
  * Seamless integration with Microsoft Teams
  * Quick and secure sharing
  * Enhance your team communication and collaboration today!

== Installation ==

1. Download and install the plugin.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin settings in `Settings > Teams Publish`.

== Usage ==

1. Go to `Settings > Teams Publish` to configure the Microsoft Teams channels.
2. When creating or editing a post, go to the editor sidebar and look for the "megaphone" button.
3. Click on the "megaphone" button to configure the sharing of the post to the specified Microsoft Teams channel.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I get the Microsoft Teams Webhook URL? =
1. Open Microsoft Teams and navigate to the channel you want to post to.
2. Click on the "More options" (three dots) next to the channel name.
3. Select "Connectors".
4. Find and configure the "Incoming Webhook" connector, which will provide you with a URL.
